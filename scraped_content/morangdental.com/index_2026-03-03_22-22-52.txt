### DOCTOR OF DENTAL MEDICINE

## Meet Dr. William Morang
Dr. William Morang, D.M.D. is a native of Athens. He graduated from Tulane University in 2005 with a double major in Cell and Molecular Biology and Spanish.
Following graduation, Dr. Morang studied anatomical sciences at the University of Georgia. During that time, he had the opportunity to assist his father, Dr. James Morang (a recently retired orthodontist of 40 years) with charity dental work at the Athens Nurses Clinic. Dr. Morang also traveled with his father on numerous mission trips to Nicaragua, Peru, and Mexico, where they provided dental work. These experiences ultimately inspired Dr. Morang to pursue a career in the dental field.
An avid sailor, Dr. Morang then went on to manage a junior sailing program in Annapolis, Maryland, for two years before attending dental school in 2008. He received his Doctor of Dental Medicine degree from the University of Louisville School of Dentistry in 2012.
Dr. Morang is a member of the American Dental Association and is the current president of the Clarke County Dental Society.  He has worked as a dentist in the Athens area since graduation from dental school.  Dr. Morang is experienced in providing everything from basic preventative dental care to cosmetic rehabilitation.  He is committed to providing custom, caring dental services to his patients.
In his spare time, he enjoys spending time with family and going to the lake to race sailboats. Dr. Morang enjoys providing charity dental care at the Athens’ Nurses Clinic on Fridays
